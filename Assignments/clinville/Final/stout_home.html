This Web site background may look familiar to residents of the Harrisville area as it is the "Stout Tan" color of our store. Stout Hardware was established in 1880. Located in Harrisville, West Virginia, it has been serving the people of Ritchie County for over 130 years. We carry a full line of plumbing, electrical, and painting supplies as well as appliances, hunting and fishing supplies, and camping gear. We focus our business on having what people really need. Our newly added RV supplies supports the pipeliners in our area as well as the camper at North Bend State Park and the nearby North Bend Lake. We stock hard to find items that are not found in the big-box and chain hardware stores.

We sell guns and ammo, appliance parts, bee supplies, bulk seeds, packaged seeds, animal feed, corn, fencing supplies, building supplies, lumber, and small engine parts. Services include small engine repair, key cutting, glass cutting, equipment rental, taxidermy drop-off, hunting and fishing licenses, custom pipe threading, and a knowledgeable staff ready and willing to help you.

As you can see, we do it all. Why go anywhere else? Check us first and get our years of experience and know-how to get you jobs done fast.
