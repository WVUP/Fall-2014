Community Involvement

The First Settlement Derby Dames is dedicated to serving the Marietta-Parkersburg community through volunteerism and fund raising.

Rink Rash for Rescues

FSDD has hosted a benefit event for the Humane Society of the Ohio Valley annually since 2012. The event features the Hades Ladies roller derby team in a home exhibition at the Washington County Fairgrounds.
