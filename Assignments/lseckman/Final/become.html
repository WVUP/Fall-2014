Join The First Settlement Derby Dames

Haven't skated in years? It doesn't matter. Roller derby is a sport wear physique doesn't matter, but it is a challenging and physical sport. What does matter is that you are female and at least 18 and you must use quad skates. Practices are held several times a week in which you will learn everything from game play to sportsmanship, basic skating techniques, falling safely to prevent injuries, and maneuvering quickly. There will be drills and endurance training. FSDD abides by WFTDA rules and regulations, even with rules and regulations it may appear to be a brawl at times, because roller derby involves fast skating, and lots of blocking with real and hard hits. That is why it is required that everyone wear the following safety gear.

Required Safety Gear:

  • Helmet
  • Mouth guard
  • Wrist guards
  • Elbow pads
  • Knee pads

So you don't have the right equipment to be a Derby Dame, not a problem; the league is also in need of referees and non-skating officials.
